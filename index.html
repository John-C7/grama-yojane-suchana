Welcome to our Rural Information Portal!

This website provides valuable information about government schemes aimed at benefiting villages. You will find links to the respective government websites where you can explore detailed information about these schemes.

Context: Rural development is a priority, and this portal serves as a bridge between rural communities and government initiatives. Stay informed and empower your village with the knowledge of available schemes.
